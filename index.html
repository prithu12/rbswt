The Rudra Baba Social and Welfare Trust, a non-governmental organization (NGO) established in Bihar. Their mission revolves around education, literacy, health, and family welfare. Specifically, they are committed to promoting a drug-free society. Inspired by the Nasha Mukt Bharat Abhiyaan (NMBA), which aims to combat substance abuse across India, the trust actively works towards creating awareness, prevention, and rehabilitation. Through their efforts, they contribute to a healthier and more empowered community

Mission

Our Rudra Baba Social Welfare trust, we are committed to empowering lives by breaking the chains of addiction. Our mission is to create a healthier, addiction-free society where individuals can thrive physically, mentally, and emotionally.

Values

Compassion: We treat every person with empathy, respect, and kindness.
Integrity: We uphold honesty, transparency, and ethical practices.
Community: We believe in collective efforts—uniting individuals, families, and society toward a common goal..

Purpose

The purpose of Rudra Baba Social Welfare Trust extends beyond treatment. We actively engage in awareness campaigns, rehabilitation programs, and preventive education. We believe that every individual deserves a chance at recovery and transformation..

Vision

We envision a community where substance abuse is replaced with hope, resilience, and well-being. Our vision is to build a drug-free environment that fosters growth, unity, and compassion.
